My Philosophy

To do what nobody else will do, in a way that nobody else can do, in spite of all we go through, is to be a nurse --Rawsi Williams

 Using my own values and the metaparadigm of professional nursing, I have discovered my own personal philosophy of nursing.  I believe nursing to be a career of compassion, caring, self-discipline, hard work, responsibility, intelligence, and honesty.  Nursing is as a profession dedicated to the wellness of human beings, and the promotion of health in a safe and healing environment.  Nursing is a dynamic process centered fully on the care of others in order to restore them to their optimal functionality.

Project 1

Free Medical Camp Camp

To make basic healthcare accessible for all, ESIC Model Hospital conducts free health camps in villages - every year. Many of these villages have no healthcare facilities or even a qualified healthcare worker. Volunteers from the hospital – Doctors,Nurses and Paramedics- are living up to our motto “To improve the quality of life”. The services that were provided at the medical camp were as follows:
1.Cardiac
2.Orthopaedic
3.Paediatric
4.Spine etc.

Project 2

Free Vaccination camp

A mobile vaccination centre was set up on a busy road in Ludhiana. Commuters and drivers got vaccinated through the mobile setup. This was an attempt to make the vaccine more accessible.

Project 3

Medical Awareness camp

A Public Health Awareness Programme was conducted by DMC & Hospital on Sunday at district Ludhiana. At this programme thorough medical check-up of 205 patients was done by team of specialist doctors from DMC & Hospital. The team constituted doctors from various specialties such as Medicine, Eye, Skin, Gynae, Urology, Dentistry, ENT, Orthopedic and Onco-Surgery. Free of cost Blood Pressure monitoring, Blood Sugar and ECG were done. The prescribed medicines were provided free by the Hospital. Patients examined were having various diseases, which they were unaware of.
